About Us

We are AMT Junk Removal

Providing Affordable Junk Removal

Here at AMT Junk Removal we stive to provide ethical, affordable and efficient service to all our customers. We specialize in residential junk removal, constuction debris pick-ups and outdoor clean-ups.

  • We provide no obligation, free estimates on site so there are no suprises!
  • We offer extremely competitive rates!
  • We work safely and quickly for your convenience!

We prioritze recycling and donation centers over landfills so yo can keep your conscience clear.

Pricing

Our rates are based on volume. You only have to pay for the space you use.

Bedload Pricing

Bedload, or heavy loads are charged at a different rate due to safety and cost. Bedload includes items such as dirt, concrete, cast-iron or roofing shingles. We can only take four yards of bedload per truck to ensure road safety for everyone.

Surcharges and Fees

All fees and surcharges are due to safety hazards or extra disposal costs.

  • 01 Fridges / Freezers / Air Conditioners / Units Containing Freon

    $35 dollars per freon containing unit.

  • 02 Tires (+$10 per tire)

    $10 dollars per tire.

  • 03 Pianos / Pool tables / Single items over 300lbs

    $50 per overweight item.

  • 04 Mattresses / Box springs / Futons

    $30 per mattress or boxspring. (two twin boxprings with king mattress counts as 1 mattress and one boxspring)

  • 05 Latex Paint

    $10 per full gallon of paint. (Six 1 gallon paint cans that are half full count as 3 gallons of paint)

  • 06 Fluorescent lights

    $0.30 per ft

  • 07 Labor Charge

    $50 per man-hour after the first two man-hours. The first two man-hours are free. So one person working two hours or two people working one hour is free.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What forms of payment do you accept?

    We accept credit cards, debit cards, mobile pay and cash.

  • What are you areas of operation?

    We currently service Kitsap County & Pierce County. If your zipcode is not available for service please contact us via phone at 360-860-5865. Bainbridge, Bremerton, Gig Harbor, Kingston, Port Orchard, Poulsbo, Silverdale, and Tacoma.

  • What are your hours of operation?

    We operate from 8am to 6pm, Monday through Sunday. You can also send us an email or leave us a voice message anytime for a quick response.

  • How soon can AMT be at my house?

    In most cases, within 24 hours and possibly even same day service.

  • What can AMT take away?

    AMT can take just about anyything we can lift into our trucks. Here is a list:

    Appliances & Electronics - stove, refrigerator, freezer, dishwasher, washer/dryer, tv's, dehumidifier, microwave, stereo, computers, monitors, printers & more.

    Furniture - couch, table, chair, mattress/boxspring, carpeting, dresser & more.

    Demolition Materials - lumber, roofing, fencing, concrete, drywall, sheetrock & more.

    Junk - boxes, books, cardboard, old tools, tires & more.

    Miscellaneous - lawn equipment, water heaters, furnaces & heavy/bulk items.

    Specialty Items - Latex Paint, Fluorescent Lights.

  • What items will AMT not take away?

    We are not able to take any hazardous materials including chemicals, gasoline, oils, non-latex paints, solvents, asbestos or asbestos containing materials, full propane tanks or any types of toxic waste. We cannot take hazardous wastes because we are not legally able to carry hazardous materials in our trucks. Please call your local city recycling center for help with disposing of any hazardous materials.
